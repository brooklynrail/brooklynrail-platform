short_bio: "**Choghakate Kazarian** is a curator and art historian based in New
  York City. Her interests are focused on artistic processes and the interaction
  between biography and artistic practice. She has curated several exhibitions
  on artists such as Henry Darger, Lucio Fontana, Piero Manzoni and Karel Appel.
  She has edited several exhibition catalogues and published on artists such as
  Marcel Duchamp, Louis Michel Eilshemius, Stéphane Mandelbaum among others. She
  has a MA in art history from Ecole du Louvre and a MA in philosophy at La
  Sorbonne. She was a curator at the Musée d’Art Moderne de la Ville de Paris
  from 2011 — 2018. She is now pursuing a Ph.D. at the Courtauld Institute of
  Art with a dissertation on the American artist Albert Pinkham Ryder."
